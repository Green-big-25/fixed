    For larger projects we visit, take measurements, and walk through design options before pricing anything in writing.

    We walk the finished project with you, answer questions, and leave a written care plan so the install lasts as long as it should.

Common Questions

Direct answers
to what people ask.

Still have questions? Call (916) 849-4844 — Mon–Sat 9AM–7PM.

How much does artificial turf cost in Elk Grove?
Artificial turf in Elk Grove typically runs $9 to $20 per square foot fully installed, depending on turf grade, base prep needs, and yard access. A small backyard (~500 sq ft) usually falls in the $5,000–$8,500 range. Full-yard projects start around $12,000.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. Green Landscaping of Sacramento is fully licensed — License #1059991. We are also BBB A+ Accredited and carry all required insurance for residential landscaping.
How long does an artificial turf install take?
Most residential installations in Elk Grove take 2 to 4 days from sod removal through final infill. Larger or sloped yards with hardscape edging may take 1 to 2 weeks. Every quote includes a written project timeline.
Do you do drainage, sprinkler repair, or maintenance?
No. We focus on installation only — turf, hardscape, retaining walls, planting, and design. We don't offer drainage systems, grading, irrigation repair, or routine lawn care. If your project needs those first, we can refer you to a specialist.
What cities do you serve?
Primarily Elk Grove, Sacramento, Folsom, Rancho Cordova, and Rancho Murieta — roughly a 25-mile radius from central Elk Grove. Call to confirm a specific address.
How fast will I hear back on a quote?
Most quote requests get a same-day response Mon–Sat 9AM–7PM. Text photos plus your address to (916) 849-4844 — that's the fastest path. You can also fill out the form below.
Is the quote really free?
Yes — every quote is free, in writing, and includes itemized scope, materials, and timeline. No deposit, no obligation, no pressure follow-up.
