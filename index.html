Common Questions

Things People Ask Us First

If your question isn't here, just call — we don't charge for answers.

How much does a typical new-build backyard cost?
Most new-construction backyards in Elk Grove and Rancho Cordova run $8,000–$25,000 depending on square footage, turf vs. concrete mix, and finish level. Quote is always free — call us with your address and we'll give you a real number.
How long does a turf and concrete project take?
Most full backyard installs are done in 2–5 working days once the materials arrive. Concrete needs cure time before heavy use (about a week), but you can walk on it the day after the pour.
Are you actually licensed and insured?
Yes — California C-27 license #1059991, fully bonded and insured. We'll send you the certificate before any crew steps on your property.
Do you handle HOA approvals?
We've worked in nearly every new-build HOA across Sacramento, Elk Grove and Rancho Cordova. We can give you the spec sheet your HOA wants and provide drawings if needed.
What kind of turf do you use?
USA-made premium turf only — pet-safe, UV-stable, with proper drainage backing. We don't use the bargain-grade stuff that flattens in a year. We'll show you actual samples at the walkthrough.
Will my concrete crack?
Properly poured, properly cured concrete with control joints in the right places does not crack visibly for many years. The reason most concrete cracks is bad sub-base prep and skipping joints — both of which we don't skip.
Can you do just turf, or just concrete?
Yes. Most homeowners want both, but we'll happily do just one. The reason we promote both together is that combining them is faster, cleaner, and usually saves money.
How fast can you respond to my call?
Same day, every time — usually within an hour during business hours. We don't do voicemail-tag like most contractors. Call by 5 PM, hear back today.
How fast can you start the actual install?
For most projects, 1–3 weeks from quote to first day on site. During spring booking season this can stretch to 4–6 weeks — call sooner rather than later.
Do you only do turf and concrete?
Yes. We do not do general landscaping, mowing, tree work, irrigation as a standalone service, or random handyman tasks. We focus on turf and concrete because doing two things well beats doing ten things average.
